About Us

Behtar Mustaqbil Foundation (BMF) is a non-governmental organisation, established in November 2004. At a time when social commentary, action, and uplifting society through common goals are most needed, the BMF is steadily proving its ability to take on the burden of educating and initiating changes for a brighter future for all. At its heart, six aims make up the foundation of BMF including ...

  • Environmental Conservation
  • Women Empowerment
  • Community Support
  • Educational Programs
  • Social Awareness
  • Health & Wellbeing

Message from the Founder

Raja Hamid, the visionary founder of BMF and the president of BMF, is the driving force behind its success. Making up your mind to accomplish something and then putting in the effort to achieve it is no easy task, especially when the goals is to establish a large-scale non-governmental organisation. Hamid did not only stay committed to his idea but also worked tirelessly to make it come true. Today, as an emerging NGO, Behtar Mustaqbil is credited to Hamid’s energy, enthusiasm and dedication. From turning this vision to a reality, to putting together a qualified team, Hamid has proven himself to be a true leader.
